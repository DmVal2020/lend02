ComputAge

ComputAge - Decentralized Science organization, that builds an ecosystem of computational biologists in longevity. Our mission is to onboard engineers into longevity, organize them into research groups, conduct research, validate hypotheses, and create a space for new scientific breakthroughs.

ComputAge Coursebook (link)

The course aims to form a systematic view of the biology of aging, and offer a holistic computational methodology for the study of this problem. We will take a detailed look at aging clock models, survival curves, Gompertz models, dynamical models as well as key aspects of the biology of aging. Students will get experience in differential gene expression or methylation analysis in the context of aging, hazard ratio models fitting and interpretation, aging clocks construction and their exploration and develop an intuition in dynamical systems modeling.

ComputAge Journal club (link)

The Journal Club provides a platform for the presentation of scientific articles, exchange of ideas, discoveries, and experiences in biology of aging. This includes both articles you've authored and any other studies of interest you would like to discuss with fellow members.
